NASA's Webb and Extreme Starburst Galaxies

Nasa's James Webb Space Telescope has provided unprecedented insights into extreme starburst galaxies.

These galaxies are characterized by exceptionally high rates of star formation.

Webb's advanced capabilities allow scientists to study the conditions that lead to such rapid star formation.

Understanding these galaxies helps researchers learn about the evolution of the universe.

The observations from Webb are key to unveiling the mysteries of galactic growth and stellar evolution.
